# C524 Certified Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt (CLSSYB)

Hands-on Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt labs for learners who want to support process improvement initiatives using DMAIC thinking, Lean waste analysis, process mapping, problem-solving tools, risk analysis, and sustainment practices.

## Course Information

- **Course Code:** C524
- **Course Title:** Certified Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt (CLSSYB)
- **Duration:** 2 days / 15 hours
- **Level:** Beginner
- **Course Registration:** [Certified Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt (CLSSYB)](https://www.tertiarycourses.com.sg/certified-lean-six-sigma-yellow-belt.html)
- **Reference:** [AALSSC Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt Study Guide](https://aalssc.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/AALSSC-study-guide-Yellow.pdf)

## Lab Requirements

- Laptop with Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or LibreOffice Calc
- Drawing tool such as PowerPoint, Draw.io, Miro, Visio, or paper templates
- Calculator
- Sticky notes or digital whiteboard
- A simple workplace process scenario, such as course registration, invoice approval, support ticket handling, onboarding, order fulfilment, or claims processing

## Suggested 2-Day Flow

| Day | Activities |
| --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Labs 01 to 05: Lean Six Sigma overview, DMAIC, Lean, waste, SIPOC, and process walk |
| Day 2 | Labs 06 to 10: value stream mapping, action planning, problem solving, risk, improvement tools, control, and readiness |
