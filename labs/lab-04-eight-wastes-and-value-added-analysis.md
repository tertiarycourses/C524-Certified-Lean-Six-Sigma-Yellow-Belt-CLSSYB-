# Lab 04: Eight Wastes and Value-Added Analysis

## Domain

Lean Principles and 8 Wastes

## Objective

Identify Lean waste and distinguish value-added work from non-value-added work.

## Tasks

1. List the 8 wastes of Lean.
2. Break the selected process into 8 to 12 steps.
3. Mark each step as value-added, business-required, or non-value-added.
4. Identify waste examples in each step.
5. Classify each waste type.
6. Estimate customer impact and team impact.
7. Prioritise the top three wastes.
8. Select one waste for further analysis.

## Deliverable

Submit a value-added analysis table and waste priority table.

## Review Questions

1. What is the difference between value-added and non-value-added work?
2. Which waste is most common in your process?
3. Which waste has the highest customer impact?
4. How can Yellow Belts help identify waste?
