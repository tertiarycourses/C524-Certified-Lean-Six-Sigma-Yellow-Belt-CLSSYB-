# Lab 1 — Yellow Belt Role, Certification Paths, and Improvement Scenario

**DMAIC phase:** FOUNDATIONS  |  **Lab type:** Core  |  **Course:** Certified Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt (CLSSYB) Training (C524)

## Objective

Explain the Yellow Belt role and select a suitable improvement scenario (A1).

## Scenario

Contoso Service Desk handles employee IT requests. Employees complain that tickets take too long to be assigned, that they must keep chasing for status, and that agents apply different definitions of what counts as "resolved". You are supporting the improvement team as a Yellow Belt.

## What you will build

A Yellow Belt responsibility table and a selected improvement scenario.

**Tools and techniques:** Belt role matrix, CSSC certification pathway, project selection criteria

## Steps

### Step 1

Define the Yellow Belt role — list at least four responsibilities and your contribution to each.

### Step 2

Compare the White, Yellow, Green, Black and Master Black Belt pathways in a table.

### Step 3

Review the Contoso Service Desk scenario: tickets are slow to be assigned and status is unclear.

### Step 4

Test your scenario against the 'good project' criteria — day-to-day work, manageable, aligned to business goals, data available.

### Step 5

Record why a Yellow Belt supports rather than leads this improvement.

## Check your work

You can state the Yellow Belt role in one sentence and justify your scenario against all four selection criteria.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your Contoso improvement package and is your reference for applying these tools at work.
