# Lab 03: Lean Thinking, Gemba, and Kaizen

## Domain

Lean Principles and 8 Wastes

## Objective

Apply Lean thinking by observing the actual process and identifying a small Kaizen improvement.

## Tasks

1. Define value from the customer's perspective.
2. Plan a Gemba walk for your process.
3. Observe or simulate the process steps.
4. Record handoffs, waiting points, repeated work, and unclear decisions.
5. Separate facts from assumptions.
6. Identify one small Kaizen opportunity.
7. Write a simple test idea.
8. Identify who should approve or support the test.

## Deliverable

Submit Gemba notes and a Kaizen idea card.

## Review Questions

1. What is Gemba?
2. Why should teams observe the actual work?
3. What makes an improvement a Kaizen idea?
4. Why should observations be factual?
