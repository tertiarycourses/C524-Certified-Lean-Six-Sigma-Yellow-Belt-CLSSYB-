# Lab 09: 5S, Standard Work, Visual Management, and Poka-Yoke

## Domain

Process Improvement Strategies and Sustainment

## Objective

Design practical improvement actions using 5S, standard work, visual management, and mistake proofing.

## Tasks

1. Choose one improvement action from Lab 06 or Lab 08.
2. Apply Sort, Set in Order, Shine, Standardise, and Sustain.
3. Draft a standard work checklist.
4. Identify one visual management idea.
5. Identify one mistake-proofing idea.
6. Explain how each idea reduces defects, waiting, overprocessing, or rework.
7. Identify who must approve the change.
8. Identify any training or communication needed.

## Deliverable

Submit a 5S plan, standard work checklist, visual management idea, and poka-yoke idea.

## Review Questions

1. What does 5S help improve?
2. Why is standard work important?
3. What makes visual management effective?
4. How does mistake proofing prevent errors?
