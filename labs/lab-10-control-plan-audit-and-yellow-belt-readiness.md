# Lab 10: Control Plan, Audit, and Yellow Belt Readiness

## Domain

Process Improvement Strategies and Sustainment

## Objective

Create a sustainment plan and confirm Yellow Belt readiness.

## Tasks

1. Select the improvement action to sustain.
2. Identify the metric that will show whether the improvement is working.
3. Define the target.
4. Assign an owner.
5. Set review frequency.
6. Create a reaction plan for missed targets.
7. Create a simple audit checklist.
8. Identify warning signs of regression.
9. Prepare a 3-minute mini storyboard presentation.
10. Complete the readiness checklist in the learner guide.

## Deliverable

Submit a control plan, audit checklist, readiness checklist, and mini storyboard.

## Review Questions

1. Why do improvements regress?
2. What belongs in a control plan?
3. How do audits support sustainment?
4. Which Yellow Belt topic do you need to revise most?
