# Lab 01: Quality Improvement and Lean Six Sigma Overview

## Domain

Background and Lean Six Sigma Overview

## Objective

Explain quality improvement, customer satisfaction, Lean, Six Sigma, and Cost of Poor Quality using a workplace process.

## Tasks

1. Choose a process to study.
2. Identify the customer and their expectations.
3. Define quality from the customer's point of view.
4. List examples of poor quality.
5. Classify each poor quality example as rework, delay, complaint, extra labour, waste, or lost opportunity.
6. Explain how Lean would improve the process.
7. Explain how Six Sigma would improve the process.
8. Write a combined Lean Six Sigma improvement statement.

## Deliverable

Submit a process quality profile and COPQ notes.

## Review Questions

1. Who defines quality?
2. Why is customer satisfaction important to process improvement?
3. What is Cost of Poor Quality?
4. How do Lean and Six Sigma complement each other?
