# Lab 08: Root Cause, Risk, and Change Readiness

## Domain

Problem Solving, Risk, and Change Management

## Objective

Use root cause tools, basic risk analysis, and change readiness thinking.

## Tasks

1. Brainstorm possible causes for your selected problem.
2. Group ideas using an affinity diagram.
3. Select one likely cause and perform 5 Whys.
4. Build a fishbone diagram.
5. Identify possible failure modes for a proposed improvement.
6. Score severity, occurrence, and detection in a simple FMEA table.
7. Calculate Risk Priority Number.
8. Identify risk actions.
9. Discuss change acceptance using Q x A = I.

## Deliverable

Submit affinity groups, 5 Whys, fishbone diagram, FMEA table, and change readiness notes.

## Review Questions

1. Why should teams avoid jumping to the first cause?
2. How does 5 Whys support root cause thinking?
3. What is the purpose of FMEA?
4. Why does acceptance affect improvement impact?
